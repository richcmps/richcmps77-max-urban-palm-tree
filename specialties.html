Our Specialties

Camphora Rx provides a wide range of pharmacy services with a focus on patient-centered care. Our specialties include:

  • Chronic disease medication management (diabetes, hypertension, COPD)
  • Medication therapy management (MTM) and comprehensive medication reviews
  • Immunizations and seasonal vaccine programs
  • Compounding for customized medication needs (where available)
  • Specialty medication coordination and prior authorization support
  • Over-the-counter recommendations and wellness counseling
  • Travel medicine guidance and vaccine recommendations

If you have specific needs not listed here, contact us and our pharmacists will work with you to find the best solution.

Volunteer Requirements & Guidelines

To ensure the safety and quality of our volunteer program, we have established the following requirements:

Basic Requirements

  • Must be at least 16 years of age (parental consent required for minors)
  • Commitment to volunteer for a minimum of 3 months
  • Ability to commit to at least 4 hours per week
  • Completion of background check (provided at no cost)
  • Attendance at orientation and training sessions
  • Professional appearance and demeanor

Training & Orientation

All new volunteers must complete our comprehensive orientation program, which includes:

  • Introduction to Camphora Rx mission and values
  • HIPAA and patient privacy training
  • Safety protocols and emergency procedures
  • Communication skills and customer service excellence
  • Role-specific training for your chosen volunteer position

Volunteer Roles in Detail

Community Health Educator

Help educate the community about medication safety, health screenings, and wellness programs. This role involves:

  • Presenting at community events and health fairs
  • Distributing educational materials
  • Answering general health questions (non-clinical)
  • Assisting with health screenings coordination

Patient Navigation Assistant

Support patients and customers as they navigate our pharmacy services:

  • Greeting and directing customers
  • Assisting with paperwork and forms
  • Providing information about pharmacy services
  • Offering companionship and support to waiting patients

Administrative Support

Provide valuable behind-the-scenes support to our pharmacy team:

  • Filing and organizing documents
  • Data entry and record management
  • Inventory assistance (non-pharmaceutical items)
  • Event planning and coordination support

Recognition & Appreciation

We deeply value our volunteers and recognize their contributions through:

  • Quarterly volunteer appreciation events
  • Annual recognition awards ceremony
  • Letters of recommendation upon request
  • Professional references for career advancement
  • Continuing education opportunities
  • Volunteer hours certification for school or community service requirements

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I volunteer if I'm a full-time student?

Absolutely! We offer flexible scheduling to accommodate students. Many of our volunteers balance their academic commitments with their volunteer work, and we're happy to provide a schedule that works for you.

Do I need medical or pharmacy experience?

No prior experience is required! We provide all necessary training. However, if you do have relevant experience, we can match you with roles that leverage your skills.

What if I can only volunteer during certain seasons?

We understand that availability can vary throughout the year. While we prefer longer-term commitments, we also welcome seasonal volunteers for specific events and busy periods.

Is there parking available for volunteers?

Yes, free parking is available in our designated volunteer parking area. Details will be provided during your orientation.

Ready to Get Started?

We're excited to welcome you to the Camphora Rx volunteer family! To begin your volunteer journey:

  1. Complete the volunteer interest form on our contact page
  2. Attend a volunteer information session (held monthly)
  3. Complete the application and background check
  4. Participate in orientation and training
  5. Begin making a difference in your community!

Join us today! Your time and dedication can make a real difference in the health and well-being of our community members. Together, we can create a healthier, more informed community.

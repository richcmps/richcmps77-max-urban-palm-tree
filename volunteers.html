Volunteer Opportunities

Published on January 5, 2026

Join Our Team

At Camphora Rx, we believe in the power of community. Our volunteer program offers meaningful opportunities to make a difference in the lives of others while gaining valuable experience in healthcare and pharmacy services. Whether you're a student, retiree, or simply passionate about helping others, we have a place for you.

Volunteer Roles

Community Outreach

Help organize health fairs, wellness events, and educational programs in the community.

Patient Support

Assist customers with navigation, provide companionship, and help with non-clinical tasks.

Administrative Help

Support our team with filing, data entry, and other administrative duties.

Benefits of Volunteering

  • Gain hands-on experience in a healthcare setting
  • Make meaningful connections with community members
  • Develop professional skills and build your resume
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your availability
  • Letter of recommendation upon completion of service

Requirements

  • Minimum age of 16 years old
  • Commitment of at least 4 hours per week for 3 months
  • Pass a background check
  • Attend a brief orientation session
  • Professional demeanor and positive attitude

How to Apply

Ready to make a difference? We'd love to hear from you! Please visit our contact page to submit your volunteer application or call us at (555) 123-4567 to learn more about current opportunities.
